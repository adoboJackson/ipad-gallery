Uncork the free Visit Napa Valley™ app today! Based on location, this app provides visitors with area accommodations, dining, things to do, wineries, wine shops and tasting rooms, offers and events. Nearby businesses are sorted by distance; search for what you’ve got in mind and save favorites you’ve discovered. The app allows visitors to make reservations and unlock special offers with the tap of the finger.
