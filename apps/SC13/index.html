The South Carolina Official Vacation Guide app transforms the print magazine into an interactive, user-friendly experience. Custom built for the iPad, this sleek and exciting app takes users on a virtual journey through the Palmetto State. Tap to set off on a video tour of the state's coastal and mountain golf courses. Swipe to see which iconic diners and music festivals draw a crowd. Take off on a Steeple Chase to explore historic churches. Videos and live links take full advantage of the tablet's agility. Everything about this app will inspire users to explore and engage with South Carolina.

Features:
  • Dual-orientation layouts allows users to navigate content easily
  • Intuitive interface delivers an immersive experience
  • Lush, visual photography brings South Carolina to life
  • Niche feature stories appeal to specific interests
  • Layered content gives an overview of the South Carolina travel experience
  • Full-page advertisements add additional videos
  • Live links encourage engagement with South Carolina through blogs and social networks
